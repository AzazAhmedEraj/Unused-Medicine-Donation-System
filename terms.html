1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using MedDonate ("the Platform"), you agree to be bound by these Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use this platform.

2. Purpose of the Platform

MedDonate is a non-profit initiative that facilitates the donation and distribution of unused, unexpired medicines from individuals and organizations to verified NGOs, hospitals, and needy patients across Bangladesh.

3. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old or registering on behalf of a registered NGO/charitable organization in Bangladesh to use this platform.

4. Medicine Donation Rules

  • Only sealed, unexpired, and properly stored medicines are accepted.
  • Donors must provide accurate expiry dates, batch numbers, and storage conditions.
  • We do NOT accept: expired medicines, opened strips, syringes, needles, refrigerated items, or controlled drugs.
  • MedDonate reserves the right to reject any donation without explanation.
